<p><p>The osteoporosis therapeutics market encompasses various treatment types aimed at preventing bone loss. Bisphosphonates, such as alendronate, inhibit bone resorption and are widely used. Hormonal therapies, including estrogen, help maintain bone density. Strontium ranelate promotes bone formation and reduces resorption, while Raloxifene, a selective estrogen receptor modulator, offers protection against fractures. Denosumab, a monoclonal antibody, targets osteoclasts to reduce bone loss. Other treatments may include calcitonin and parathyroid hormone, providing additional options for patients.</p></p>
