<p><p>The Circular Steel Hollow Section (CHS) market comprises two primary types: Welded Steel Hollow Section and Seamless Steel Hollow Section. Welded CHS is fabricated by welding steel plates, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility. It is widely used in construction and structural applications. Seamless CHS, produced from solid rounds of steel, provides enhanced strength and reliability, making it ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types are essential in various industries, including construction, automotive, and manufacturing, driven by their structural performance and durability.</p></p>
