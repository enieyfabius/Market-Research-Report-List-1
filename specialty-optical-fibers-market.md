<p><strong>The Specialty Optical Fibers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode Specialty Optical Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The specialty optical fibers market is typically divided into two main types: multi-mode specialty optical fiber and single-mode specialty optical fiber. Multi-mode specialty optical fiber is designed to carry multiple light modes or rays simultaneously, ideal for short-distance communication and applications requiring high bandwidth. In contrast, single-mode specialty optical fiber allows only one mode of light to propagate, ensuring minimal signal loss over longer distances, making it suitable for long-range telecommunications and high-speed data transmission applications.</p></p>
