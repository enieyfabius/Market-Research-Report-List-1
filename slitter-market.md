<p><strong>The Slitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Slitting</li><li>Extrusion Slitting</li><li>Round Slitting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The slitter market comprises three main types: flat slitting, extrusion slitting, and round slitting. Flat slitting is used for cutting wide rolls of material into narrower strips, commonly employed in paper and film industries. Extrusion slitting involves slicing extruded materials, particularly in the production of plastic films and sheets, ensuring precise width control. Round slitting, on the other hand, utilizes circular blades to achieve clean cuts, primarily in applications involving flexible materials like textiles and rubber, enhancing productivity and efficiency.</p></p>
