<p><p>The Microelectromechanical Systems (MEMS) microphones market is segmented into two main types: analog and digital. Analog MEMS microphones convert acoustic signals into continuous electrical signals, offering simplicity and lower cost, ideal for basic audio applications. In contrast, digital MEMS microphones provide digital output using integrated circuitry, enabling enhanced performance, noise reduction, and advanced features like beamforming. Digital types cater to high-fidelity audio and smart devices, making them suitable for applications in smartphones, wearable technology, and hearing aids.</p></p>
