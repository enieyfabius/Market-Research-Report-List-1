<p><p>The Desk and Office Cleaner market is categorized into two primary types: aerosol cleaners and solution cleaners. Aerosol cleaners are packaged in pressurized containers, making them easy to spray on surfaces for quick and effective cleaning. They often provide an even distribution and quick drying time. Solution cleaners, on the other hand, are liquid formulations that require manual application using cloths or mops. They are versatile and can be adjusted in concentration, making them suitable for various cleaning tasks in office environments.</p></p>
