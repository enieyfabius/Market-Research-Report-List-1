<p><p>The automotive cleaner and degreaser market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based formulations. Water-based cleaners use water as the main solvent, offering environmentally friendly options that are less toxic and safer for users. They effectively remove dirt and grease, though they may require more scrubbing. Solvent-based cleaners, on the other hand, contain organic solvents, providing powerful grease-cutting capabilities and faster drying times. However, they can be more hazardous and less eco-friendly, often requiring careful handling.</p></p>
