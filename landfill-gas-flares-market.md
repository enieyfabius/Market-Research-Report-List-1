<p><p>The landfill gas flares market consists of two primary types: candlestick flares and enclosed ground flares. Candlestick flares are open-air devices designed to burn off excess landfill gas, providing a visible flame and ensuring safe disposal of methane. In contrast, enclosed ground flares are more advanced systems, utilizing a closed combustion chamber for greater efficiency and reduced emissions. Both types are crucial for managing landfill methane, minimizing environmental impacts, and complying with regulatory standards in waste management.</p></p>
