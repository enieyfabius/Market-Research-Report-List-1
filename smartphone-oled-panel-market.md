In contrast, Flexible OLED panels are crafted from bendable materials, allowing for innovative designs like curved displays and foldable smartphones, enhancing user experience. The increasing demand for sleek and lightweight devices drives the growth of flexible OLED technology, while rigid OLED remains popular for standard models.</p></p>
