<p><strong>The Automotive OEM Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tempered</li><li>Laminated</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive OEM glass market comprises primarily three types: tempered, laminated, and others. Tempered glass is heat-treated for enhanced strength and shatter resistance, commonly used in side and rear windows. Laminated glass consists of layers of glass and a polyvinyl butyral interlayer, providing safety features by preventing shattering upon impact, primarily used in windshields. The "others" category includes specialty glass types, such as tinted and acoustic glass, offering additional functionalities like UV protection and noise reduction for automotive applications.</p></p>
