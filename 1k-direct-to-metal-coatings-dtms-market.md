<p><p>The 1K Direct-to-Metal Coatings (DTMs) market is segmented into waterborne and solventborne types. Waterborne DTMs utilize water as a primary solvent, offering lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and improved safety. They are typically easier to clean and environmentally friendly. Solventborne DTMs, on the other hand, use organic solvents and provide excellent adhesion and durability, making them suitable for harsh environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, emphasizing performance and compliance with regulatory standards.</p></p>
