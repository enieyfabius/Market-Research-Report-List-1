<p><strong>The Automobile Sheet Spring Assembly Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Springs</li><li>Mono-leaf Springs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile sheet spring assembly market comprises primarily two types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of metal, providing higher load capacity and durability, making them suitable for heavy vehicles and off-road applications. In contrast, mono-leaf springs feature a single-layer design that offers lighter weight, simplicity, and improved ride comfort, often preferred in passenger cars. The choice between them depends on vehicle type, intended use, and performance requirements.</p></p>
