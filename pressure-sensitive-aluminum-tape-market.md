<p><p>The pressure-sensitive aluminum tape market is categorized into single coated and double coated tapes. Single coated tape consists of an aluminum foil backing with adhesive on one side, offering good temperature resistance and durability for various applications. Double coated tape features aluminum foil on both sides with adhesive in between, providing enhanced bonding capabilities and versatility for industrial and construction uses. Both types are valued for their reflective properties and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for insulation and sealing tasks.</p></p>
