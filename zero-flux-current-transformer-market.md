<p><p>The Zero Flux Current Transformer market includes two main types: busbar-mounted and panel-mounted transformers. Busbar-mounted transformers are integrated directly onto busbars in power distribution systems, providing efficient current measurement with minimal space requirements. In contrast, panel-mounted transformers are installed within switchgear or control panels, offering flexibility and ease of maintenance. Both types are essential for accurate current monitoring and protection in electrical systems, contributing to enhanced safety and reliability in power distribution applications.</p></p>
