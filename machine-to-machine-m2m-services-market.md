<p><p>Machine to Machine (M2M) services encompass various connectivity types, each suited for specific applications. Wi-Fi is ideal for high-bandwidth needs in localized environments, while Ethernet provides stable wired connections for industrial settings. Zigbee is designed for low-power, short-range communication, making it perfect for IoT devices. Satellite is essential for remote locations lacking traditional infrastructure. Power-line communication utilizes existing electrical wiring for data transmission, offering a practical solution in urban settings where installation of new wiring is challenging.</p></p>
