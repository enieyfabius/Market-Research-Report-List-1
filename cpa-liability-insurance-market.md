<p><p>CPA liability insurance encompasses various market types, primarily Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance. D&O insurance protects corporate directors and officers from legal actions related to their managerial decisions, safeguarding personal assets against claims of negligence or wrongdoing. In contrast, E&O insurance specifically covers professionals against claims arising from errors, omissions, or inadequate work in their services, ensuring financial protection against client lawsuits. Both types play crucial roles in risk management for professionals.</p></p>
