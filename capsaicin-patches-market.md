<p><p>The Capsaicin Patches market encompasses various delivery methods for enhanced pain relief. Transdermal absorption allows the active ingredient to penetrate the skin, providing localized relief. Sonophoresis utilizes ultrasound waves to increase skin permeability, facilitating deeper absorption. Iontophoresis employs electrical currents to drive ionized drugs through the skin, improving efficacy. Microneedle transdermal delivery involves tiny needles that create microchannels in the skin, enabling rapid and effective drug delivery while minimizing discomfort. Each method offers unique benefits for pain management and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
