<p><strong>The Suture Anchor System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Suture Anchor System</li><li>Non-absorbable Suture Anchor System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Suture Anchor System market is primarily categorized into two types: absorbable and non-absorbable suture anchors. Absorbable suture anchors are designed to degrade in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal and minimizing foreign material in tissues. In contrast, non-absorbable suture anchors remain permanently in the body, providing durable support for long-term stability in various orthopedic and surgical procedures. Each type serves specific clinical needs based on the intended duration of support and the patient's recovery process.</p></p>
