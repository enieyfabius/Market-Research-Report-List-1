<p><p>Infrared tube heaters are categorized primarily into two types: single-stage and two-stage infrared tube heaters. Single-stage infrared tube heaters operate at a fixed output level, providing consistent heating for spaces where uniform warmth is needed. In contrast, two-stage infrared tube heaters offer adjustable output levels, allowing users to switch between high and low heat settings, which enhances flexibility and energy efficiency. Both types are utilized in various applications, including industrial, commercial, and residential heating solutions.</p></p>
