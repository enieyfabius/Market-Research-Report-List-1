<p><strong>The Hydrorefining Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-hydrorefining Catalyst</li><li>Hydrorefining Catalyst</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydrorefining catalyst market can be categorized into two main types: pre-hydrorefining catalysts and hydrorefining catalysts. Pre-hydrorefining catalysts are used in the initial stages of the refining process, where they prepare feedstock by removing impurities and enhancing the quality of the crude oil. In contrast, hydrorefining catalysts are employed in the main refining process to facilitate hydrogenation reactions, removing sulfur, nitrogen, and other undesirable components, thereby producing cleaner fuels and improving overall product efficiency.</p></p>
