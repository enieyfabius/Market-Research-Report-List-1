<p><ul><li>Continuous Lasers Systems</li><li>Pulsed Lasers Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser systems market is primarily divided into two types: continuous laser systems and pulsed laser systems. Continuous laser systems emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring steady output, such as cutting and welding in industrial processes. In contrast, pulsed laser systems generate short bursts of high-energy light, suitable for applications that need precision and high peak power, such as medical procedures and materials processing. Each type serves distinct industries and applications, leveraging their unique characteristics.</p></p>
