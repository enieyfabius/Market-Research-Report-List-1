<p><p>The Airport Ground Treatment Market encompasses various methods to enhance soil stability and support airport infrastructure. The Sand Drain Method involves inserting sand columns to accelerate drainage. The PVD Method utilizes prefabricated vertical drains for quicker consolidation. Vibro Compaction densifies granular soils using vibration to improve load-bearing capacity. Pneumatic Flow Mixing Method combines soil with additives for strength. Jet Grouting injects cementitious materials into the ground for stabilization. Other methods include various innovative techniques tailored to specific soil conditions and project requirements.</p></p>
