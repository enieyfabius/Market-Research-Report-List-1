<p><p>Monomethylammonium nitrate (MMAN) is utilized in various explosive formulations. Water gel/slurry explosives combine water, ammonium nitrate, and additives, offering flexibility and safety in various conditions. Emulsion explosives are made by emulsifying ammonium nitrate in oil, providing high energy and stability, ideal for mining and construction. Seismic explosives, which include MMAN, are designed for geophysical applications, such as subsurface mapping and oil reservoir exploration, delivering precise energy release for effective ground penetration and analysis.</p></p>
