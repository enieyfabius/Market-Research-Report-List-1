<p><strong>The Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Manual</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steering market types include Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), and Manual Steering. EHPS combines electronic controls with hydraulic systems, enhancing performance and energy efficiency. HPS relies solely on hydraulic pressure generated by a pump, offering a traditional feel but less efficiency. Manual steering provides direct mechanical connection, requiring more driver effort but enhancing feedback and control. Each type addresses different vehicle requirements and driver preferences, influencing overall driving dynamics and comfort.</p></p>
