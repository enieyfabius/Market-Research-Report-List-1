<p><p>Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) units are crucial for treating wastewater by removing suspended solids and impurities. The market features two primary types: open tank and plate pack systems. Open tank DAF units utilize a large basin for flotation, allowing for high flow rates and easy maintenance. In contrast, plate pack systems employ closely spaced plates to enhance the flotation process, improving efficiency and reducing footprint. Both types are widely used in various industries for effective water treatment solutions.</p></p>
