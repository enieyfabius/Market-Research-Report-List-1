<p><p>The lithium-ion battery equipment market encompasses three primary types: pretreatment, cell assembly, and post-processing. Pretreatment involves initial material preparation, ensuring the quality and suitability of components for battery production. Cell assembly focuses on constructing the battery cells, integrating electrodes and electrolytes in a controlled environment to optimize performance. Post-processing includes finalizing the battery units, which may involve diagnostics, testing, and packaging, ensuring the batteries meet safety and efficiency standards before reaching the market.</p></p>
