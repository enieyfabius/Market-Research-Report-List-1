<p><p>The insulated panel system market comprises various types, including PU sandwich panels, EPS sandwich panels, and mineral wool sandwich panels. PU sandwich panels offer excellent thermal insulation and durability, making them ideal for energy-efficient buildings. EPS (expanded polystyrene) sandwich panels are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for various construction applications. Mineral wool sandwich panels provide fire resistance and sound insulation, making them preferred for industrial and commercial structures. Additionally, the “Other” category encompasses emerging materials and specialized applications, catering to diverse construction needs.</p></p>
