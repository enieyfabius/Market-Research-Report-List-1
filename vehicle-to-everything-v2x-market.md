Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) enhances safety by alerting pedestrians of approaching vehicles. Vehicle-to-Device (V2D) allows vehicles to interact with smart devices. Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) enables electric vehicles to interact with the power grid for energy sharing. Vehicle-to-Cloud (V2C) provides cloud connectivity for data exchange and analytics.</p></p>
