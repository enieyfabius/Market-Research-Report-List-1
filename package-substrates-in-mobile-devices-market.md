<p><p>Package substrates in mobile devices are essential for connecting semiconductor chips to the circuit board, enhancing performance and miniaturization. Key types include FCCSP (Flip Chip CSP), which offers high-density interconnections; WBCSP (Wafer-Level Bump CSP), known for its compact size; SiP (System in Package), integrating multiple components; BOC (Ball on Chip), providing efficient heat dissipation; and FCBGA (Flip Chip Ball Grid Array), enabling high-performance applications. Each type is tailored to meet specific needs in the mobile device market.</p></p>
