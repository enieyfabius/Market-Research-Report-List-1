<p><p>Power Line Communication (PLC) chips for smart meters utilize existing power lines to transmit data efficiently. OFDM (Orthogonal Frequency Division Multiplexing) chips enable high data rates and robust performance over noisy lines. HPLC (High-Speed Power Line Communication) chips are designed for fast, reliable communication, ideal for smart grid applications. BPSK (Binary Phase Shift Keying) chips provide a simpler modulation scheme, ensuring effective data transmission over shorter distances, making them suitable for cost-sensitive deployments in utility and home applications.</p></p>
