<p><p>The Biometric Identification Lens market includes three main types: Optical Zoom, Digital Zoom, and Other. Optical Zoom lenses utilize physical movement of glass elements to magnify images, providing high-quality, detailed biometric capture. Digital Zoom, conversely, enlarges an image through software, often resulting in lower resolution and quality. "Other" encompasses various specialized lenses or technologies that may not fit the first two categories but are still used in biometric identification applications, offering unique capabilities tailored to specific needs.</p></p>
