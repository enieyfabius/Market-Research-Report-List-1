<p><p>The blood tumor drugs market encompasses various treatment types, including chemotherapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, and others. Chemotherapy uses cytotoxic drugs to kill rapidly dividing cancer cells but can affect healthy cells as well. Targeted therapy focuses on specific molecular targets associated with cancer, minimizing damage to normal cells. Immunotherapy harnesses the body’s immune system to recognize and attack cancer cells, improving overall response. Other market treatments may include hormone therapies or stem cell transplants, offering additional options for blood tumor patients.</p></p>
