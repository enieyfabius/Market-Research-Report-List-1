<p><p>The vapor degreasing solvents market includes several key types: NPB (n-Propyl Bromide), TCE (Trichloroethylene), and Perc (Perchloroethylene), each used for cleaning metal and industrial components. NPB is favored for its low toxicity and effectiveness in removing oils, while TCE is known for its strong degreasing properties but raises health concerns. Other solvents encompass a range of alternatives, expanding options for environmentally conscious cleaning solutions.</p></p>
