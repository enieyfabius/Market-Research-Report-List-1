<p><p>The electric drill market is primarily divided into two types: corded and cordless drills. Corded drills are powered directly through an electrical outlet, offering consistent power and higher torque, making them ideal for heavy-duty tasks. In contrast, cordless drills operate on rechargeable batteries, providing greater portability and convenience, which is suitable for tasks in locations without power access. Both types cater to different user preferences, with corded models favored for sustained use and cordless models preferred for versatility and ease of movement.</p></p>
