<p><p>The bio-based propylene glycol (PG) market comprises two main types: bio-based 1,2-propylene glycol and bio-based 1,3-propylene glycol. Bio-based 1,2-propylene glycol, derived mainly from renewable sources, is widely used in pharmaceuticals, food, and cosmetics due to its low toxicity and excellent moisturizing properties. Conversely, bio-based 1,3-propylene glycol is typically utilized in industrial applications, such as antifreeze and de-icing products, owing to its superior performance in extreme temperatures. Both types support sustainability by reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
