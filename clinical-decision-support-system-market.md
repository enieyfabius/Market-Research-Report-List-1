<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>EHR-CDSS</li><li>EHR-CDSS-CPOE</li><li>CDSS-CPOE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) markets can be categorized into four main types. Standalone CDSS operates independently, providing clinical guidance without integration into other systems. EHR-CDSS integrates CDSS functionalities directly within electronic health records, enhancing workflow efficiency. EHR-CDSS-CPOE combines CDSS with computerized physician order entry, streamlining medication and treatment decisions. Lastly, CDSS-CPOE focuses on decision support specifically for order entry processes, facilitating better clinical decisions during prescribing and ordering, ultimately improving patient care and safety.</p></p>
