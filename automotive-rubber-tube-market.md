<p><p>The automotive rubber tube market comprises two main types: non-reinforced rubber tubes and metal-reinforced rubber tubes. Non-reinforced rubber tubes are flexible and lightweight, primarily used for fluid transfer and air circulation in vehicles. In contrast, metal-reinforced rubber tubes incorporate metal elements for enhanced strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure applications such as coolant and fuel systems. Both types are crucial for ensuring efficient vehicle performance, safety, and longevity in various automotive applications.</p></p>
