<p><strong>The Ultra High Frequency AFM Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Arrow Tip</li><li>Rotating Tip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra High Frequency Atomic Force Microscopy (AFM) probe market primarily consists of two main types: Arrow Tip and Rotating Tip. Arrow Tip probes are designed for high sensitivity and enhanced resolution, featuring a sharp, pointed geometry that allows for precise topographical imaging of surfaces at the nanoscale. On the other hand, Rotating Tip probes utilize a rotational motion to improve the lateral resolution and enable dynamic imaging capabilities, making them suitable for various applications in material science, biology, and nanotechnology.</p></p>
